YouTube Resume · Chrome Extension

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  Maintained by Emerald Zhu  ·  Permalink

The short version: YouTube Resume stores your video playback positions locally on your device. Nothing leaves your browser. No accounts, no tracking, no servers. Ever.

What this extension does

YouTube Resume is a Chrome extension that saves your playback position in YouTube videos so you can resume exactly where you left off — even after closing a tab, restarting your browser, or experiencing a crash. It works automatically in the background and requires no configuration.

Data we collect

YouTube Resume stores the following information, and only this information:

  • The YouTube video ID of videos you watch (e.g. dQw4w9WgXcQ)
  • Your playback position in seconds at the time of the last save
  • The total duration of the video in seconds
  • A timestamp of when the position was last saved

This data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API. It is never transmitted, shared, or accessible to anyone other than you.

Permissions explained

YouTube Resume requests exactly two permissions from Chrome:

  • storage — required to save and retrieve your playback positions locally on your device.
  • Host access to youtube.com — required to run the extension's code on YouTube pages so it can interact with the video player.

No other permissions are requested. The extension cannot read your browsing history, access other tabs, or interact with any website other than YouTube.

Data storage and retention

All data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. This storage is private to the extension and is not accessible to websites, other extensions, or any external party.

The extension stores progress for a maximum of 200 videos. When this limit is reached, the oldest entries are automatically removed to make room for new ones. You can manually delete all stored data at any time using the "Clear saved progress" button in the extension popup.

Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data.

Third-party services

YouTube Resume does not integrate with or communicate with any third-party services. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

Children's privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected or transmitted, the extension poses no privacy risk to users of any age.
