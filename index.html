YouTube Resume

Automatically resume YouTube videos exactly where you left off. Invisible until needed, reliable always.

Invisible by Design

No configuration, no accounts, and no visible interface during normal playback. It just works quietly in the background.

Crash Recovery

Browser crash, accidental tab close, or restarting tomorrow? Your progress is saved exactly where it was.

100% Local Privacy

Zero network calls. No analytics. All progress data is stored directly on your machine.
